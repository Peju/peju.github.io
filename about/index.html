Who am I?

Hi, I'm Peter! I'm currently an associate product manager at The Lifetime Value Co. (f.k.a BeenVerified) with specializations in product design and UX research. I work on the Bumper.com brand, which is helping empower people to make the most informed decisions when buying used vehicles! Check out my resume.


Throughout my life, I've always been interested in building and creating new things to help improve peoples' lives. Bringing people delight is something I find very gratifying.


I graduated early (Dec 2019) from New Jersey Institute of Technology with a B.Sc in Business Information Systems with Magna Cum Laude honors. I previously worked The Social Interaction Lab where I did various HCI (Human-Computer Interaction) work. In addition I've interned at two Fortune 100 companies and a startup, checkout my resume or LinkedIn! Besides my interest career interests, I shoot street photography, produce music, and occasionally write for fun. I also love playing tennis and basketball!

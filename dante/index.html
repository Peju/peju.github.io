Analysis

_____________________________________

An interesting mention is there are no prophets from the Old Testament in this circle, Dante has no explanation for their absence.

Some of the sinners are famous seers from classical myths such as Amphiaraus and Tiresias (both seers of Greek Mythology) They faced God's poetic justice for trying to see to far in the future, their punishment fits their sin, now they can only see what's behind them.

Diviners - Derived from the Latin divinare, which means "to forsee" or "to be inspired by a god" To practice divination is to uncover hidden knowledge by supernatural means.

Astrologers- Astrology is the interpretation of an assumed influence the stars (and planets) exert on human destiny.

Magicians- Were believed to have gained their power from Satan and other demonic forces. Magic was considered to be used as a way to mock God by trying to replicate his miracles.

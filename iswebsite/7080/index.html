1980's-1990's: History of the Internet

Above is two five-year-old students try out 'Cecil,' the name given to the computer at Woolston Infants School in Cheshire, England Dec 1980

With the continued development of building what we know as the internet, package switching became a new method of transferring data to various stops along its path. This ensured that data could be sent through various ways if one way was closed since it was influenced by the design of a honeycomb. Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol (TCP/IP) is the standard for computer communication (1973). Soon various polticial figures such as Queen Elizabeth, Jimmy Carter, etc. were using the internet in addition to there being 500k computers in the U.S. (1979). Domain name system is established i.e .com, .edu.org (1984).

10's-Future: History of the Internet

Reference

With the internet anyone can access information at nearly instataneous rates, which has been unprecedented in human history. The internet has also facilitated opportunities for business and professional developments to blossom. The demand to advertise products of any business companies become a less of a major problem then it once way, as companies develop and model their website to target consumers to their product. The internet has been a major advancement in the Information Age, enabling anyone to cause an influence globally for the good - or bad. However, the new frontiers of cybersecurity and privacy have been trending topics because of the complex relationship between the people and government entities.

1990s - 2000s: History of the Internet

The 90s saw the browser wars between Microsoft and Netscape. It saw Windows become a domimant OS. Apple's rise to fame in the begining of the 21st century was starting to take form. But most of these events could be overshadowed by something bigger. It was hailed as the second gold rush, with investors looking to drop their funds for any business with a .com domain. Everyone found out at the same time that most of these startups had no real plan or competitive advantage. Then the bubble burst. Millions of lost dollars resulted in much more hesitant investing in internet companies. But the ones that made it out alive ended up being some of the biggest names in Silicon Valley.

1950s - 1960s: History of the Internet

The core formation of the internet happened during the 1950s, starting when the USSR launched Sputnik, the first man-made satelite. Just a year later, Bell Labs invented the modem - a device to modulate and demudulate signals over a wire. The US government also created ARPA, the Advanced Research Projects Agency.

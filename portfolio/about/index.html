Who am I?

Hi, I'm Peter! I'm an aspiring product manager. Currently I'm a PM intern at Prudential Financial and a lead research assistant at The Social Interaction Lab


Throughout my life, I've always been interested in building and creating new things to help make people's lives better. Combining my interests of team collaboration, business strategy, and technology made me realize product management is my passion.


I study Business Information Systems at New Jersey Institute of Technology. I previously interned at MassMutual as a PM, and taught programming at iD Tech Camps. Besides my interest in product management, I shoot B/W street photography, produce music, and occasionally screenwrite for fun. I also enjoy playing tennis and basketball!

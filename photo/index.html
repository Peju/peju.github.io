Who am I?

An ardent hobbyist who enjoys capturing moments that last a lifetime. While I am an amateur photographer, I am constantly improving in software editing or learning new photography techniques. My shots are done wherever I travel. I'm located in the NYC area and am a freshman at NJIT. The inspiration for "Last Dream" comes from the idea that photographs never show the beginning or end, but always jump cuts to that moment, similarily to dreams.

Who am I?

An ardent photgrapher with a passion to capture unique moments. The inspiration for "Last Dream" comes from the idea that photographs never show the beginning or end, but always jump cut to that moment, similarily to dreams. Dreams are unique as well, you may never have the same moments similarily to photos, they are snaps of life - captured, preserved and sometimes lost.

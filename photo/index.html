Who am I?

An ardent hobbyist who enjoys capturing moments that last a lifetime. While I am an amateur photographer, I am constantly improving in software editing or learning new photography techniques. The inspiration for "Last Dream" comes from the idea that photographs never show the beginning or end, but always jump cut to that moment, similarily to dreams.
